Pho

The ingredients and tools you are going to need are

  • Noodles
  • Beef
  • Green onions
  • Water

Once you have all your items ready, follow these instructions in the order they are given!

  1. Boil the noodles.
  2. Cut the green onions.
  3. Boil the water in a pot and put in beef and pork bones to create broth.
  4. Put the boilion hot soup in a bowl with noodles and put fresh beef slices on top of the noodles.
