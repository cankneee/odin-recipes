Scrambled Eggs Items

The ingredients and tools you are going to need are

  • Eggs
  • Pan
  • Oil
  • Whisk

Once you have all your items ready, follow these instructions in the order they are given!

  1. For the first step we are going to crack open the eggs into a bowl.
  2. Afterwards we will whisk it until it is nice liquidy.
  3. Turn on the fire for the pan and put oil on top. Afterwards put the eggs in.
  4. Voila! You know have scrambled eggs!
