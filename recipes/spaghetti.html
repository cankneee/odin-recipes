Spaghetti

The ingredients and tools you are going to need are

  • Noodles
  • Meatballs
  • Green onions
  • Sauce of your choosing

Once you have all your items ready, follow these instructions in the order they are given!

  1. Boil the noodles.
  2. Cut the green onions.
  3. Cook the meatballs in a pan on medium fire.
  4. Put the noodles on a plate and add the sauce and meatballs on top.
